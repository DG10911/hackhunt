Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Aligned with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023

This policy explains what personal data HackHunt India collects, why, and your rights.

1. What we collect

  • Account data: name, email and profile photo (via Google or GitHub sign-in), and optionally college, year, skills, achievements, GitHub/LinkedIn links and interests that you choose to add.
  • Activity: events you save, pages you view, and any Team Finder posts you create.
  • Technical & security data: your IP address, approximate location (city/region/country derived from the IP), browser/device type (user-agent) and timestamps. We use this only to keep the Service secure — detecting and blocking abuse, attacks and fraud.
  • We do not collect passwords (sign-in is handled by Google/GitHub), we do not track you across other websites, and we do not sell your data or show ads.

2. Why we use it (purpose)

  • To run your account and show your saved events across sessions.
  • To personalize recommendations and Team Finder.
  • To send deadline reminder emails — only if you save events (you can opt out anytime).
  • Security: to detect, prevent and respond to attacks, abuse, spam and unauthorized access (this is why we process IP, location and device data, as a legitimate use).

2a. Cookies & local storage

We use your browser's local storage to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. Note that Cloudflare, our security and delivery provider, may set essential cookies to protect the Service from attacks.

3. Consent

By creating an account you consent to this processing for the purposes above. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account, which removes your personal data.

4. Your rights (DPDP)

  • Access & correction: view and edit your data in your profile.
  • Erasure: request deletion of your account and data.
  • Grievance: contact our grievance officer below.

5. Storage & sharing

Data is stored in our application database. Team Finder contact details you post are shown publicly to other users (that's their purpose). We don't share your data with advertisers. We use trusted third-party providers only to operate and protect the Service — currently Railway (hosting), Cloudflare (security, DDoS protection and content delivery), Google/GitHub (sign-in), an IP geolocation lookup (for security), and an email provider (for reminders). These providers process data on our behalf under their own security terms.

6. Retention

We keep account data while your account is active — delete your account to remove it. Security logs (IP, location, device, activity) are kept for a limited period for abuse prevention and then removed automatically.

7. Contact / Grievance Officer

Email privacy@hackhunt.xyz for any data request, correction, deletion, or complaint, and we will respond within a reasonable time.

This is a student project template, not legal advice. For commercial use, have a lawyer confirm DPDP compliance and appoint a Data Protection Officer if required.
