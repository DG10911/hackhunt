Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Aligned with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023

This policy explains what personal data HackHunt India collects, why, and your rights.

1. What we collect

  • Account data: name and email (via sign-up, Google or GitHub), and optionally college, year, skills, achievements, GitHub/LinkedIn links and interests that you choose to add.
  • Activity: events you save, and any Team Finder posts you create.
  • We do not collect passwords (sign-in is via Google/GitHub or a simple email profile), and we do not sell your data.

2. Why we use it (purpose)

  • To run your account and show your saved events across sessions.
  • To personalize recommendations and Team Finder.
  • To send deadline reminder emails — only if you save events (you can opt out anytime).

3. Consent

By creating an account you consent to this processing for the purposes above. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account, which removes your personal data.

4. Your rights (DPDP)

  • Access & correction: view and edit your data in your profile.
  • Erasure: request deletion of your account and data.
  • Grievance: contact our grievance officer below.

5. Storage & sharing

Data is stored in our application database. Team Finder contact details you post are shown publicly to other users (that's their purpose). We don't share your data with advertisers. We use third-party providers only to operate the Service (e.g., email delivery, hosting).

6. Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. Delete your account to remove it.

7. Contact / Grievance Officer

Email privacy@hackhunt.example for any data request or complaint.

This is a student project template, not legal advice. For commercial use, have a lawyer confirm DPDP compliance and appoint a Data Protection Officer if required.
